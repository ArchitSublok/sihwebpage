Mental Wellness Check-in

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings. Your well-being matters.

1. Over the last week, how often have you felt calm and peaceful?

2. How well have you been able to manage stress in your daily life recently?

3. How would you describe your energy levels lately?

4. How connected have you felt to friends or family this past week?

5. Have you been able to enjoy your usual hobbies and interests?

6. How has your sleep quality been recently?

"You are not your illness. You have an individual story to tell. You have a name, a history, a personality. Staying yourself is part of the battle."
